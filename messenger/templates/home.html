A service that allows users send emails by sending a sms to a AT's shortcode (Useful when there's no internet)

  • Create a Shortcode from your AT's account
  • Add this URL as your Callback URL: https://puzher.herokuapp.com/listener/
  • Use the sandbox to send SMS to your shortcode.
  • SMS should be inform of: Can you cancel all my meetings? receiver@gmail.com , the content of the mail will be sent to the mail included in your text.
