A service that allows users send emails by sending a sms to a Africa Talking's shortcode OR Dailing a USSD (Useful when there's no internet)

  • Create a Shortcode OR USSD Number from your Africa Talking's account
  • For SMS: Add this URL as your Callback URL: https://puzher.herokuapp.com/listener/
  • For USSD: Add this URL as your Callback URL: https://puzher.herokuapp.com/ussd-listener/
  • Use the sandbox to send SMS to your shortcode OR Dial the USSD Code
  • SMS/USSD text should be inform of: Can you cancel all my meetings? receiver@gmail.com | The content of the mail will be sent to the mail included in your text.
